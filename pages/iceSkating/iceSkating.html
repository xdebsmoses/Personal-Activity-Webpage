DEBORAH TAKES ON ICE-SKATING

The Beginning...

When i first put my right foot on the ice, i immediately knew it wasnt going to be an easy task to complete
and it wasnt long after i fell.. infront of everyone. A nice gentelman helped me
this was when i decided to go to the kids section and start there until i felt confident enough to move on. which was hard as i watched other professionals do it with ease

The Process

To start skating, push off with one foot. As you glide forward, bring the foot you pushed off with back down onto the ice, then use your other foot to push off. Continue alternating feet to keep skating forward, and hold your arms out to your sides a little bit to help you balance.
